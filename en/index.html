The engine

Five layers. Deterministic.

What separates a credible audit from a list of tool warnings: a single scope, systematic verification, a synthesis that prioritises.

01
1 crawl

Reconnaissance and scoping

A single upfront crawl: inventory of pages and subdomains, stack detection, mapping of forms and endpoints. Agents receive a scope, they do not rediscover it fifteen times.

02
parallel

Fan-out of the fifteen agents

Each active agent runs in parallel on the source code and the live production, with its own framework and tools.

03
anti false-positive

Adversarial verification

Every finding is challenged by a skeptic agent: real or false positive, reproducible, severity justified by evidence. Majority vote on critical ones.

04
impact / effort

Dedup, scoring, prioritisation

The same problem often surfaces via security AND perf AND SEO. We merge it, score it on unified severity, prioritise it by impact / effort.

05
roadmap

Synthesis

Global health score, executive summary, roadmap quantified in person-days and in euros of risk and gain. Ready for the decision-maker.

Why it beats the existing tools.

SEMrush, Ahrefs, Screaming Frog, Lighthouse, Snyk, OWASP ZAP: each covers one box. Panoptic unites them, white-box, with verification.

01 / The decisive edge

Traceability to the line of code

Competitors crawl your URL black-box. Panoptic connects your repo: a slow Core Web Vital does not become a score, it becomes this component, this file, this line. Nobody else links the symptom in production to its cause in the code.

02

Verified findings

Every issue is challenged by a skeptic agent before the report. Few false positives, the number-one pain of current tools.

03

Business framing

Every finding translated into money: GDPR fine risk, SEO loss, conversion gain. What the decision-maker buys.

04

Continuous, not one-shot

Automatic re-audit on every deployment, regression detection, history and trend.

05

Competitive benchmark

Your site compared to three competitors on each of the fifteen dimensions.
